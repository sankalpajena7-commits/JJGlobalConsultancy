About Us

A Global Engineering Consultancy Built on Trust

JJ Global Consulting Pvt. Ltd. is an international engineering consultancy that brings together world-class talent from London, Mumbai, Singapore, Myanmar, and Bhubaneswar to deliver high-performance building engineering solutions.

Supported by a multidisciplinary delivery team with deep global project experience, we help clients optimise building performance through sustainable design, digital engineering, and integrated project delivery methodologies.
