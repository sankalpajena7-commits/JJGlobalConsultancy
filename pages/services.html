BIM Services

Building Information Modelling across all project stages

Digital Engineering

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

We are a BIM-first consultancy. Every project we deliver is modelled to the appropriate Level of Detail (LOD) using industry-leading platforms including Autodesk Revit and Navisworks. Our BIM specialists work as seamlessly integrated extensions of your design team.

From BIM Execution Plan (BEP) preparation to Federated Model Management and COBie data delivery, we cover the full BIM workflow in compliance with ISO 19650.

  • BIM Execution Plan (BEP) preparation
  • LOD 100–500 MEP model production in Revit
  • Federated model coordination and clash detection
  • 4D construction sequencing and 5D cost integration
  • ISO 19650 compliant Common Data Environment (CDE)
  • COBie data delivery for FM handover
  • BIM training and client support
