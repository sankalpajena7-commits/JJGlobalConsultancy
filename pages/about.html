Our Story

From a Vision to a Global Engineering Force

JJ Global Consulting Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular vision: to provide world-class engineering consultancy services that bridge international standards with local insights. What began as a focused MEP engineering practice has grown into a multidisciplinary global consultancy, delivering innovative solutions across five continents.

Headquartered across London and Mumbai, with representation in Singapore, Myanmar, and Bhubaneswar, we combine the rigour of UK engineering standards with the agility and depth of our delivery teams in India and Asia.

Our name — JJ Global Consulting — reflects our commitment to a joined-up, journey-based approach to project delivery: from concept through to operation.

The People Behind the Projects

Our Team

A multidisciplinary group of experts working seamlessly to deliver integrated solutions.

🏛️

Architects

Our architects create functional, aesthetically inspiring, and sustainable spaces that respond to the unique needs of every client. By combining creativity with technical expertise, they develop designs that maximize efficiency, enhance user experience, and integrate seamlessly with engineering disciplines throughout the project lifecycle.

⚙️

Mechanical Engineers

Our mechanical engineers specialize in designing high-performance HVAC and mechanical systems that deliver optimal comfort, energy efficiency, and operational reliability. Their solutions are tailored to meet international standards while ensuring sustainability and lifecycle cost optimization.

⚡

Electrical Engineers

Our electrical engineering team designs safe, reliable, and energy-efficient power distribution, lighting, ELV, and backup power systems. By integrating advanced technologies and smart solutions, they ensure seamless building operations and enhanced occupant safety.

💧

Plumbing Engineers

Our plumbing engineers develop efficient water supply, drainage, sewage, stormwater, and water conservation systems. Their designs prioritize sustainability, operational efficiency, water quality, and compliance with applicable codes and standards.

🔥

Fire Protection Engineers

Our fire protection engineers design comprehensive fire detection, alarm, suppression, hydrant, sprinkler, and life safety systems. Every solution is developed to protect lives and property while complying with national and international fire safety regulations.

🏗️

Civil Engineers

Our civil engineers provide practical and cost-effective engineering solutions that support strong, durable, and sustainable infrastructure. From site development to structural coordination, they ensure projects are executed with quality, precision, and technical excellence.

📊

Estimation Engineers

Our estimation engineers prepare accurate quantity take-offs, cost estimates, BOQs, and tender documentation. Their expertise helps clients make informed financial decisions, optimize project budgets, and achieve greater cost certainty from concept to completion.
