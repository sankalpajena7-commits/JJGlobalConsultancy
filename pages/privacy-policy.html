Privacy Policy

How JJ Global Consulting Pvt. Ltd. collects, uses, and protects your information.

Note: This is a draft privacy policy provided as a starting point. It has not been reviewed by a lawyer and should not be treated as legal advice or a compliance guarantee. Please have this reviewed by a qualified legal professional — particularly for GDPR (UK), DPDP Act (India), and PDPA (Singapore) compliance — before relying on it publicly.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who We Are

JJ Global Consulting Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") is an international engineering consultancy with offices in London, Mumbai, and Bhubaneswar, and representative offices in Singapore and Myanmar. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through this website.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or organisation name
  • Project location and details of your enquiry

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data, payment details, or information from children through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your enquiry, discuss potential projects, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Processors

Our contact form is processed using Web3Forms, a third-party form-delivery service, which forwards your submission to our email inbox. Web3Forms may briefly process your data as part of this delivery. We do not use any other third-party analytics or advertising services at this time.

5. Cookies

This website uses a minimal, functional cookie/local-storage flag to remember that you have dismissed our cookie notice. We do not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. If this changes (for example, if we add website analytics in the future), this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry information only as long as necessary to respond to your request and maintain business records, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at info@jjglobal.co.in.
